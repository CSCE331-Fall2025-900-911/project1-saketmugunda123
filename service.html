North South Foundation

The North South Foundation is a non-profit organization dedicated to fostering educational excellence and providing opportunities for students. Founded in 1989, this volunteer-driven foundation has a dual mission: to promote academic achievement among Indian students in the United States and to offer scholarships to students in India.

I had the opportunity to volunteer as a judge for the North South Foundation, contributing directly to their mission of pursuing academic excellence. During my volunteering, I was responsible for evaluating student performances in both the spelling bee and the math bee. This role involved assessing participants on their subject knowledge, critical thinking, and presentation skills. It was inspiring to witness the impressive dedication and talent of the young students, and I was proud to play a small part in an organization that nurtures the intellectual growth of the next generation.

Round Rock Serving Center

The Round Rock Serving Center is a non-profit organization dedicated to providing support to struggling individuals and families in the community. For over 35 years, this volunteer-driven charity has served as a safety net for the community. Its primary services include a food pantry, a thrift store providing clothing and household goods, and financial assistance programs for utilities, rent, and prescriptions.

I had the opportunity to volunteer at the Round Rock Serving Center, contributing directly to their mission of supporting community members facing hardship. During my volunteering, I was responsible for sorting incoming donations and assisting clients in the food pantry. This role involved organizing food items to stock the shelves and helping families navigate the pantry to select nutritious groceries for their households. It was a humbling experience to see the direct impact of this work, and I was proud to play a small part in an organization that provides vital resources for our neighbors in need.

Educationist Tutoring

Educationist Tutoring is a free tutoring service that provides personalized learning solutions for students. The organization's mission is to create achievement and build confidence by offering customized, one-on-one tutoring for those who lack resources. Focusing on students from elementary through high school, its services cover core subjects like math, science, and language arts, as well as standardized test preparation. The center prides itself on creating tailored educational plans that address each student's unique learning style and academic goals.
