North South Foundation

Volunteer Judge | Spelling Bee & Math Bee Competitions

The North South Foundation is a non-profit organization dedicated to fostering educational excellence and providing opportunities for students. Founded in 1989, this volunteer-driven foundation has a dual mission: to promote academic achievement among Indian students in the United States and to offer scholarships to students in India.

  • Role: Volunteer Judge for spelling bee and math bee competitions
  • Responsibilities: Evaluated student performances on subject knowledge, critical thinking, and presentation skills
  • Impact: Contributed to nurturing the intellectual growth of the next generation
  • Experience: Witnessed impressive dedication and talent of young students competing at high levels

Round Rock Serving Center

Volunteer | Food Pantry & Donation Support

The Round Rock Serving Center is a non-profit organization dedicated to providing support to struggling individuals and families in the community. For over 35 years, this volunteer-driven charity has served as a safety net for the community.

  • Services: Food pantry, thrift store, and financial assistance programs
  • Role: Volunteer for food pantry operations and donation sorting
  • Responsibilities: Organized food items, stocked shelves, and assisted families in selecting nutritious groceries
  • Impact: Directly supported community members facing hardship and food insecurity
  • Experience: Gained firsthand understanding of community needs and the importance of local support networks
